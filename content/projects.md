**iOS Note-Taking App** | SwiftUI + GRDB + Cloudflare Workers

- AI-powered question suggestions
- Merge/Split card recommendations

### Tech Stack
- **Frontend**: SwiftUI, iOS 17+
- **Database**: GRDB (SQLite)
